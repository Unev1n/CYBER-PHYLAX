Verify the Website ✅ Official government domain (e.g. *.gov.in in India) ✅ Correct spelling of the website name ✅ HTTPS (🔒) ✅ No unexpected requests for payment or OTP during login ✅ Opened from the official website or app, not from a random email, SMS, or social media link 📧 2. Be Cautious with Emails and SMS Don't trust a message just because it mentions a government department. Check: Is the sender's address official? Does the message create urgency? Does it ask you to click a link immediately? When in doubt, visit the official website directly instead of using the link in the message.
🌐2 . Avoid Search Engine Ads Scammers sometimes promote fake websites through ads. Safer options: Type the official web address yourself. Use a bookmark. Follow links from official government sources.
💰 3. Be Wary of Payment Requests Be suspicious if you're unexpectedly asked to: Pay a "processing fee" Pay to release a refund Pay to claim a government benefit Always confirm payment instructions on the official website.
